ThredUp Cart Hoarding

ThredUp cart hoarding/refresher for ThredUp.com helps you auto buy/add/keep items in your cart past their reserved expiration timer. This tool automatically auto adds and/or refreshes your cart adding/keeping products reserved in your cart so that no one else can buy them but you. Perfect for snagging those high-demand items before anyone else does!
